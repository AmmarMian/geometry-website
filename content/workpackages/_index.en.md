The chair is organised as a pipeline: new geometric <em>features</em> to represent multivariate time series (WP1), new geometric <em>learning models</em> to classify and fuse them (WP2), and new geometric <em>generative models</em> to denoise, interpolate and predict (WP3). Everything is evaluated on real, public remote-sensing datasets, and released as an open toolbox. Frugality is measured, not assumed — energy consumption is tracked with tools such as CodeCarbon.
